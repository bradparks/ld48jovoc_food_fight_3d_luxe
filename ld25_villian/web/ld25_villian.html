Welcome to Evil Lair created by Joel Davis for LudumDare 25.

FPS

How to play:
The goal is to kill an agent in your Lair Room. Press 'Expand my Base' to add a room at random to your base and launch the next agent. Or, press 'Bring it on' to launch the next agent without building anything. But be careful, if you kill all the agents without any of them reaching your lair, then you won't be able to reveal your evil plan, and you will lose. Or, if an agent makes it through your lair alive, they will be able to take down your base and defeat you.
